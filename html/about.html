Nick Spilker

Owner, Builder & Sales

Nick has over 10 years of experience designing and building homes and has a degree in business management. Nick will work with you to design your custom home floor plan, whether you choose a traditional, modern or contemporary floor plan. Nick will work with you to maximize your budget and make the luxury features you want affordable. He will work directly with you from the beginning when the floor plans are drawn up to the end when he hands you the keys. His goal is to build you the custom home you've always wanted.

Clark Spilker

Owner, Manager

Clark Spilker is a highly accomplished builder. He has designed and constructed seven St. George Parade of Homes, is the former President of the Home Builder Association, and is the former Chairman of The Parade of Homes. He has been building homes in the St George area since 1976. He attended the University of Utah and studied Business Management and Finance. He has served his country in the Armed Services. He is married to his lovely wife Janet and they are the proud parents of five children. With 40 years of experience building locally, Clark has the knowledge and skills in architecture, design and construction to draw any floor plan you will need. Clark is ready to help you design the perfect floor plan and start building your dream home. Clark will personally draw up your floor plans for your future home.
