Clark Spilker

Owner, Manager

Clark Spilker is a highly accomplished builder. He has designed and constructed 7 St. George Parade of Homes, is the former President of the Home Builder Association, and is the former Chairman of The Parade of Homes. He has been building homes in the St George area since 1976. He attended the University of Utah and studied Business Management and Finance. He has served his country in the Armed Services. He is married to his lovely wife Janet and they are the pleased parents of 5 children. We have first hand experience not only with the many clients we have built for but also our own personal experience. Clark has a skill set that can set your dreams into reality as he designs and constructs your future home.
