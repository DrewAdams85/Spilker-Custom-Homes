About
Spilker Homes

A Spilker Custom Home is an investment that will produce life-long returns. The quality, craftsmanship, professionalism and service that the Spilkers build into each and every one of their Custom Homes will last a life time. Personal supervision and service are the active ingredients that separate the Spilkers from other builders.

Spilker Custom Homes has been building custom homes in the St. George area since 1976. All of the new innovative ideas, systems, products and materials available will be at your disposal when you work with the Spilker Design Build Team.

Spilker Homes is a home builder specializing in custom and luxury homes. You can be assured, what comes first with the Spilkers, will be meeting all of your needs, desires and expectations. As we develop your house plans you will be involved every step of the way.
